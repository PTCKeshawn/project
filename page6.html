Ways We Can Help Solve The Climate Crisis

  • Save Energy at Home:

    • Replace regular bulbs with energy-saving CFLs and use energy-efficient appliances.

    • Adjust thermostat settings and use programmable controls to save heating/cooling energy.

    • Unplug devices or use power strips to cut standby electricity use.

  • Get Around on Less:

    • Walk, bike, carpool, or take public transportation whenever possible.

    • Drive smart: avoid idling, maintain your car, and combine trips.

    • Choose fuel-efficient or hybrid vehicles; reduce air travel and buy carbon offsets if you fly.

  • Consume Less, Conserve More:

    • Buy only what you need; choose reusable or secondhand items.

    • Recycle and compost to reduce waste and greenhouse gas emissions.

    • Eat less meat and buy locally produced food to cut energy use and pollution.

    • and ofcourse!

  • Be Someone Who Wants Change:

    • Educate yourself and others about global warming and sustainable living.

    • Support eco-friendly companies and vote for leaders who prioritize the environment.

    • Encourage your community (schools, workplaces, neighborhoods) to adopt green practices.
